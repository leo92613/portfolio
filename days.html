Dia De Los Holos

Introduction Video

Description

This is Media Research Lab's Halloween-theme Holojam-based VR experience.
Participants wearing untethered VR headsets will get to be ghouls, ghosts and skeletons who draw in the air, play musical instruments and dance hard enough to wake the dead. Everyone will be dancing together on a future reality Day of the Dead float which will be traveling through a celebrating virtual city.
I worked as Unity Developer in the team.
